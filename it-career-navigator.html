How to Land Your IT Job

Smart strategies to stand out and succeed in the competitive IT job market.

🗂️

Build a Strong Portfolio

Your portfolio is your #1 asset. Employers want to see what you can build, not just what you claim to know.

  • Host everything on GitHub — keep it active
  • Include 3–5 well-documented real projects
  • Add a personal website with live demos
  • Show the problem you solved, not just code
🔗

Network Strategically

Over 70% of tech jobs are filled through connections. Building your network is as important as building skills.

  • Optimize your LinkedIn profile (keywords matter)
  • Join Discord/Slack communities in your niche
  • Attend local meetups and tech conferences
  • Engage with creators and experts in your field
📝

Craft a Winning Resume

Your resume must pass ATS scanners and impress human reviewers. Keep it focused and results-driven.

  • Use keywords from the job description
  • Quantify achievements ("reduced load time by 40%")
  • Keep it to 1 page for entry-level roles
  • Tailor it for every application you submit
🎯

Ace Technical Interviews

Preparation is everything. Tech interviews follow a predictable structure — learn to navigate it confidently.

  • Practice 50–100 LeetCode problems (Easy/Medium)
  • Study system design basics (for senior roles)
  • Prepare STAR-format behavioral answers
  • Always ask thoughtful questions at the end
